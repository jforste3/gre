## Dates

1st-3rd century CE

## Garden Description

This house, the largest in *insula* III (lots 2,3,4,9,10), rivals the House of the Cascade in size and richness of mosaics. A semicircular basin extended into the garden on the S and E sides, each decorated with a marine scene.
