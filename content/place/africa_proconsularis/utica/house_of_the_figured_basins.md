## Dates

2nd-4th century CE

## Garden Description

The house was located in the *insula* III. The rectangular courtyard, in the SW corner on the axis of the great hall (XI), paved during the last period may have been a garden earlier. It had a semicircular basin with a geometric design in the bottom. The wall and the brim were figured.
